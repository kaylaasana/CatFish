How to Play

Objective of the game: Go fishing to add fish into your inventory and sell them in order to buy random cat facts!


Cast rod button

Click the “click rod” button to start fishing!

Reel in button

You will need to click the “reel in” button 5 times in order to catch your fish. Be careful and pay attention, it will move around the page!

Success/failure

If you succeed in catching the fish, you will have to drag the photo into your backpack. If you fail, you can try again.

Selling the fish

Click on your backpack to view your inventory. There will be a sell button next to each fish and see the amount in your wallet go up.

Buying a cat fact

Click on your store to meet the cat sales-animal. Click the “buy random cat fact” button in order to generate and enjoy your random cat fact!

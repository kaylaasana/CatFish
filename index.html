How to Play

Objective of the game: Go fishing to add fish into your inventory and sell them in order to buy random cat facts!


Cast rod button

Click the button to start fishing!


Reel in button

You will need to click the button 5 times in order to catch your fish. Be careful and pay attention, it will move around the page!


Success/failure

If you succeed in catching the fish, you will have to drag the photo into your backpack. If you fail, you can try again.


Selling the fish

Click on your backpack to view your inventory. There will be a next to each fish and see the amount in your wallet go up.


Buying a cat fact

Click on to meet the cat sales-animal. Click the button in order to generate and enjoy your random cat fact!


Who is CJ and how to sell to him?

Every once in a while our friend CJ from Animal Crossing will pop-up on your page. CJ loves fish and will buy from you at a higher rate than usual. Make sure you click him when he appears to take advantage of the extra money!
